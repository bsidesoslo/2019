About

We're a Norwegian non-profit in the early stages of planning BSides Oslo 2019, and excited that we're already receiving interest and questions. BSides is about being good to the community so we want to share the details we have so far, even if they are not finalized yet. We also want to let you know who's behind this and how you get in touch if you have questions or would like to help out.

We're working with the support of the kind and experienced folks at securitybsides.org to bring a new kind of infosec event to Oslo. The plan is to start with a 1-day, 1-track event with the hope of expanding in the future. The date will be May 23rd, 2019, the Thursday following Paranoia 2019. This is in keeping with the common BSides approach of being adjacent to larger, commercial infosec events. We're currently making the final touches to our budget and hoping to announce the venue shortly. We've also launched our call for presenters (CFP) which will be open until March 8th, 2019, and about to officially start fundraising.

If you'd like to stay current with updates, please follow us on Twitter

CFP

Our Call for Presenters is open until March 8th, 2019.

We want to hear from brand new speakers, seasoned speakers, and everyone in between! We're open to submissions on a wide range of information security and privacy topics. Here are a few suggestions:

  • Network security.
  • Web Application security.
  • Mobile security.
  • Virtualization and cloud computing.
  • Innovative attack / Defense strategies.
  • Forensics / Malware.
  • Embedded device security / Internet of things.
  • Artificial intelligence safety and security.
  • Open source intelligence.
  • Biometrics.
  • European Train Control System / European Rail Traffic Management System.
  • Real world cases, responsibly disclosed.

The BSidesOslo Team

The BSidesOslo team are members of the local infosec community who felt the need for an accessible, inclusive event that is focused on learning and sharing. The BSides framework is a perfect fit. While individual BSides events have a focus on serving and representing their local communities, adopting this framework also ties us to an international network of diverse, independent events that seek to create opportunities to advance the infosec conversation beyond the mainstream.
